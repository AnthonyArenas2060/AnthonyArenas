About Me

I am a Master's student in Astrophysics at the Institute of Nuclear Science, UNAM. My work focuses on data analysis, stellar simulations, the search for substellar companions (brown dwarfs and exoplanets), and the characterization of binary systems. Passionate about solving scientific problems through the use of advanced computational tools and theoretical modeling. I have strong skills in programming and data visualization. Currently, I am conducting stellar and planetary evolution simulations using the MESA evolutionary code.
